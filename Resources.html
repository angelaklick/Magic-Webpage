Additional Sources

I've listed some other websites that would be beneficial to check out.


Visit Twitch for a great site to watch videos. People upload videos of them playing the game and you can see in action how cards are used. If you're interested in playing tournaments, then MTGTop8 has all the information you need. They list tournaments for every kind of deck. Two excellent websites that players of all skill levels should visit are Star City Games and Channel Fireball . Both provide articles by professional players talking about current and upcoming changes and a store where you can view and purchase cards. MTGSalvation is an online forum. The link provided will take you to discussions on modern, which is the specific deck that I play(Death and Taxes). I've also provided a YouTube video giving a overview of Death and Taxes.
